About Us

Here's my Doctor

Here's my Doctor is a platform for patients as well as the hospitals. The patients can search for any hospital or any doctor across the country, view their duty chart and availability and even book appointments with them. The hospitals can add doctors, modify their duties and view their appointments and can approve or re-schedule them as well. It provides a vast number of services ranging from viewing the availability of doctors, appointment booking to booking ambulances in case of emergency situtations
